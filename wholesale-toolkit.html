Wholesale Partner Toolkit

Everything a wholesale partner needs to represent Theory correctly. The coffee, the brand, and the standards — delivered together.

The Partner Promise

We don't just ship coffee. We ship the entire experience. Training, brand assets, equipment guidance, and an open line to our roastery. Your success is our reputation.

What We Provide
01
Freshly Roasted Coffee
Roasted to order, shipped within 24 hours. Single origins, blends, and seasonal rotations. You choose the lineup or we curate it for you.
02
Barista Training
On-site or virtual training for your team. Dialing in espresso, pour-over technique, milk steaming, and our quality standards. We come to you.
03
Equipment Guidance
Grinder calibration, machine recommendations, and water quality consultation. We help you build a setup that does the coffee justice.
04
Brand Collateral
Shelf talkers, menu boards, origin cards, and digital assets. Everything your customers need to understand what they're drinking.
05
Seasonal Releases
First access to limited releases and seasonal single origins. Keep your menu dynamic without the sourcing work. We handle the curation.
06
Dedicated Support
A direct line to our team. Order changes, quality feedback, recipe adjustments — handled by people who know coffee, not a call center.

Partner Brand Guidelines
Do
Use our provided logo files at approved sizes
Display origin information for each coffee
Tag @theorycoffee in social media posts
Serve within 21 days of roast date
Contact us with quality concerns immediately
Don't
Modify the logo colors, proportions, or type
Rebrand or repackage our coffee under another name
Serve coffee older than 30 days from roast
Mix our beans with other roasters' coffee
Use our brand in paid advertising without approval
